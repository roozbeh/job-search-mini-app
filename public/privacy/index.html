Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026  ·  iPronto Systems LLC

ResuMatch ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by iPronto Systems LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using the ResuMatch app, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

  • Resume content — text extracted from the file you upload, used to generate AI analysis and job matches.
  • Job preferences — titles, locations, salary range, job type, remote preference.
  • Authentication token — your Agnic OAuth access token, used to authenticate API calls on your behalf. We store a hashed (SHA-256) version as a session key; the token itself is not stored.
  • Saved and dismissed jobs — your in-app interactions, stored to restore your session.
  • Usage logs — server-side logs (IP address, request timestamps) retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Analyze your resume and generate improvement suggestions using AI (Agnic API / OpenAI models).
  • Search for and rank job listings that match your resume and preferences (SerpAPI).
  • Save and restore your session across devices.
  • Improve app reliability through anonymized usage logs.

We do not sell your data. We do not use your resume for advertising or share it with employers without your explicit action.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Agnic (agnic.ai) — AI model provider. Your resume text is sent to Agnic's API to generate analysis. Agnic's privacy policy applies to that processing.
  • SerpAPI (serpapi.com) — Job listing data provider. Your job search query (titles, location, preferences) is sent to SerpAPI. No personal identity or resume text is shared.
  • MongoDB Atlas — Cloud database where your session data (resume text, preferences, saved jobs) is stored.

4. Data Retention

Your session data (resume, preferences, saved jobs) is retained until you request deletion. Server access logs are retained for 30 days.

5. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — request complete deletion of your data at any time.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.

To exercise any right, email support@ipronto.net with subject "Privacy Request". We will respond within 5 business days.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS). Database access is restricted to our backend service. We do not store your OAuth token in plaintext — only a SHA-256 hash is used as a session key.

7. Children's Privacy

ResuMatch is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
