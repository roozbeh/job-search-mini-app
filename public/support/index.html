Frequently Asked Questions

How does resume analysis work?

Upload your resume (PDF or text) and our AI evaluates it for ATS compatibility, readability, and content quality. You get a score and specific improvement suggestions for each section.

How are jobs matched to my resume?

ResuMatch compares your resume's skills, experience, and preferences against job listings and surfaces the ones most likely to result in an interview — not just keyword matches.

What job types and locations are supported?

Full-time, part-time, contract, and internship roles across locations worldwide. You can filter by remote, hybrid, or on-site and set a minimum salary threshold.

Is my resume stored securely?

Yes. Your resume data is encrypted in transit and stored securely in our database. It is never shared with employers or third parties without your explicit action.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email us at support@ipronto.net with the subject "Delete My Account" and we will remove all your data within 5 business days.

The app isn't loading or I'm seeing an error — what do I do?

Try force-closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, check your internet connection, then contact us at support@ipronto.net with a description of the error.

Privacy & Data

Your resume and personal data are used solely to power your job search experience within the app. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Authentication is handled securely via Agnic OAuth. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support@ipronto.net.
